Tea Tastings Workshop

10th Aug 2024
4pm - 7pm

The tea tasting workshop offers you a journey into the world of tea, where you’ll immerse yourselves in the rich aromas, flavors & traditions of this ancient beverage. Led by experienced tea experts, the workshop provides a comprehensive exploration of various tea types, brewing techniques & sensory experiences.

The workshop begins with an introduction to the history and cultural of tea, tracing its origins from China to its global spread evolution. Through engaging storytelling and captivating visuals, you’ll gain insights into the fascinating journey of tea from leaf to cup.
